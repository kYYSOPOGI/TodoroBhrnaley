School of Computer Science

In order to meet the new challenges of the 21st century Information Technology, as well as to achieve the ambition that industrialization be promoted by modern and sophisticated devices, Arellano University is committed to training talents of the new generation in computing and Information Technology. Our goal is to produce graduates who will have the correct belief in life and the spirit of patriotism and dedication, adhering to the school's motto of judiciousness and fulfillment, having a strong scientific and technological exploration and innovation desire, and inheriting the good culture of unity, diligence, and innovativeness.

Our school upholds high moral quality, strong professional basis, good capability, and swift adaptation.

Vision

Our vision is to provide accessible, responsible, up-to-date, and flexible Computer Science and IT programs that will produce highly competent graduates for the global market.

Mission

The mission of the school is to provide an excellent, comprehensive, and industry-centered environment to prepare students for careers in the IT industry.

Goals and Objectives

The college aims to attain the following goals:

  • To produce graduates who will be competent and efficient information, industry, and technology professionals.
  • To develop and offer computer courses attuned to the needs of business society as a whole.
  • To integrate the values of social responsibility, discipline, honesty, creativity, teamwork, and integrity.
  • To network with government and non-government organizations.

The School of Computer Science, in consonance with the objectives of the Commission on Higher Education, expects the student of the College:

  • To develop analytical and logical thinking.
  • To apply different programming skills and techniques in developing simple to complex programs.
  • To employ acquired knowledge in making decisions and dealing with real-life problems.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Accreditation Level: PACUCOA Level III Accredited

The BS Computer Science program includes the study of computing concepts and theories, algorithmic foundations, and new developments in computing. The program prepares students to design and create algorithmically complex software and develop new and effective algorithms for solving computing problems.

The program also includes the study of the standards and practices in Software Engineering. It prepares students to acquire skills and disciplines required for designing, writing, and modifying software components, modules, and applications that comprise software solutions.

Objectives:

  • Train students in the design of efficient, reliable, and quality software products.
  • Prepare students to become full-grown software engineers and researchers.
  • Encouraged students to explore their research interests and discover innovative technology that will help shape the future of Computer Science Research.
